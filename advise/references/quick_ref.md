# Advise - Quick Reference

## Triggers
- "advise"
- "recommend"
- "what should I do"
- "what do you recommend"

## 9 Steps
1. DEFINE QUESTION - Understand what needs recommending
2. GATHER INFO - Web search, memory, Hindsight, mega-dive
3. COMPARE OPTIONS - Score matrix (price, quality, speed, ease)
4. WEIGHT FACTORS - What's important to Jeem
5. MAKE RECOMMENDATION - Clear top choice
6. EXPRESS CERTAINTY - 🟢 High (80%+) confidence
7. INCLUDE CAVEATS - What could go wrong
8. PROVIDE ALTERNATIVES - 2-3 options
9. NEXT STEPS - Action items

## Key Points
- Always give alternatives, not just one choice
- Express confidence level explicitly
- Include caveats
- Connect to Jeem's priorities
